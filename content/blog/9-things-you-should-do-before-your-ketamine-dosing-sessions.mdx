## **1\. Choose an Expert Ketamine Provider**

Consulting with clinics staffed by psychiatrists and anesthesiologists with depression-specific expertise will lead to practices based on evidence and [support safe patient observation](https://www.health.harvard.edu/blog/ketamine-for-treatment-resistant-depression-when-and-where-is-it-safe-202208092797?utm). Places accredited by such recognised bodies should have qualified staff trained to respond to both psychological and hemodynamic side effects. The treatment experience of team members who utilise ketamine for anxiety and depression means they can prevent additional issues throughout anxiety treatment.

## **2\. Complete A Thorough Pre‑treatment Assessment**

The assessment combines psychiatric evaluations with medical history verification alongside baseline laboratory tests to both detect treatment restrictions (eg, uncontrolled hypertension or psychotic disorders) and develop symptom-specific infusion doses. Standard assessment scales, among them PHQ-9 and GAD-7, help measure depression and anxiety intensity while providing both customised dosage approaches and treatment [results monitoring tools](https://www.va.gov/formularyadvisor/DOC_PDF/CRE_Ketamine_Infusion_for_Treatment_Resistant_Depression_Rev_Jul_2022.pdf?utm).

## **3\. Prepare Your Body and Mind**

Sleep improvements, combined with proper hydration and nutrition intake in the pre-infusion period, enhance ketamine effectiveness while reducing the chance of [nausea or headache occurrence](https://www.principiumpsychiatry.com/blog/how-to-make-the-most-of-your-ketamine-infusion/?utm). You can manage stomach discomfort after fasting for 2–4 hours before the session without compromising vital energy levels, while mental preparation through intention setting and relaxation practices allows for focused therapeutic discovery. ## **4\. Integrate Psychotherapy During and After Infusion**

The combination of medication effects through Ketamine-Assisted Psychotherapy (KAP) uses expert guidance to help patients achieve valuable emotional discoveries during dissociative states, followed by actionable [therapeutic practices post-treatment](https://www.pacificneuroscienceinstitute.org/blog/trip/maximizing-the-benefits-of-ketamine-therapy-through-integration/?utm). Therapists who specialise in integration allow clients to understand new memories while changing harmful beliefs, which results in extended resilience benefits past the basic drug effects.

## **5\. Address Co‑Occurring PTSD and Anxiety**

Many patients with treatment‑resistant depression also struggle with PTSD or high baseline anxiety. Your provider can optimise how to use ketamine for PTSD and depression with personalised treatments after they have knowledge about your complete healthcare picture, including previous traumatic experiences. The evidence shows that [**Ketamine for treatment-resistant depression**](/ketamine-therapy-for-depression) works better, which makes it potentially useful for mixed depression cases.

## **6\. Leverage the NMDA Receptor and Ketamine Mechanism**

As an NMDA [receptor](/post/how-does-ketamine-work) blocker and ketamine antagonist, ketamine prevents glutamate inhibition, which activates both AMPA receptors and mtor signalling alongside BDNF upregulation to drive quick synaptogenesis in the prefrontal [cortex and hippocampus](https://www.verywellmind.com/what-is-ketamine-infusion-therapy-5194302?utm) areas. Sub-anaesthetic infusions of ketamine at 0.5 mg/kg IV over 40 minutes provide optimal efficacy without intolerable side effects because of the mechanism explained by this cascade of events.

## **7\. Reinforce Neuroplasticity Post‑Infusion**

Secure post-infusion activities have the power to enhance the results of [ketamine treatments](/locations/online-at-home-ketamine-therapy-in-new-york) in combination with neuroplasticity effects. The combination of regular aerobic workouts to boost BDNF levels with mindfulness techniques, which lower stress-based neurotoxicity, helps you solidify [synaptic gains](https://www.painmedicineconsultants.com/blog/ketamine-therapy-for-depression-the-ultimate-guide-2024?utm). After ketamine treatment, access to well-balanced nutrition and learning activities, including brain games and skill training, both assist in the development of new neural pathways within the brain. 

‍

## **8\. Plan Maintenance Dosing and Ongoing Symptom Tracking**

The fast impact of Ketamine lasts 2–4 weeks until booster infusions are planned in order to maintain benefits while avoiding excessive exposure. Your provider should help you transition your treatment from acute (2-3 infusions per week) to a maintenance dosing schedule that requires infusions either monthly or every other week. Symptom tracking using diaries and standardized scales like PHQ-9 and GAD-7 will determine the timing of treatments. Tracking apps notify patients and their treatment teams when depression symptoms begin to reappear before the symptoms develop into a full relapse event. 

‍

## **9\. Engage Support Networks and Lifestyle Interventions**

Standing with others in a community, such as support groups or peer networks, creates an environment where members can exchange both motivational support and practical treatment information to handle the process of managing anxiety and depression with ketamine. Distractions such as smartphone usage or phone call should be minimized because they interrupt internal concentration during infusions. Developing curated music playlists dedicated to [emotional exploration helps therapy](https://www.advancedbrainbody.com/blog/how-to-have-the-best-ketamine-treatment-experience/?utm). Social groups that take place after sessions offer participants opportunities to exchange learnings and maintain commitment to their continued recovery efforts.

## Frequently asked questions

### Should I eat before ketamine therapy?

It is generally recommended to fast for two to four hours before a ketamine session to reduce the likelihood of nausea, which is one of the more common side effects. If you do eat beforehand, choose something light and easy to digest, such as fruit or a small salad, and avoid heavy, greasy, or rich foods. Your provider will give you specific pre-treatment instructions based on your route of administration and medical history.

### What should I wear during a ketamine session?

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is recommended so that you can relax fully without physical restriction during your session. Many patients prefer layers, as body temperature can fluctuate slightly during the experience. Avoid anything tight, constrictive, or distracting, and consider having a blanket nearby for added comfort.
