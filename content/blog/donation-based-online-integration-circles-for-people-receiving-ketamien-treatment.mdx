As you navigate your ketamine experiences, interaction plays a crucial role in your healing journey. Ideally, working with an integration psychotherapist who has experience and training in ketamine-assisted therapy is the best option. However, this may not be feasible for everyone, especially if you come from backgrounds that are not commonly shared among psychotherapists. Additionally, multiple psychotherapy sessions can be cost-prohibitive for many. To address this, we provide information about online integration circles that operate on a donation basis. The information about providers and services contained on this website does **not** constitute endorsement or recommendation by Isha Health. It is your responsibility to verify and investigate providers and services.We endeavor to keep this information current, but if you come across any errors, or if you are aware of other donation-based integration circles, please let us know by emailing info@isha.health.

## 1\. Isha Health Ketamine Integration Circle

Host: Isha Health

When: 6:30 pm - 8 pm PST on the First Wednesday and the third Wednesday of every month

How often: Every two weeks

Where: Virtual

Where to sign up: [https://www.meetup.com/ishahealthketamineintegrationcircle/](https://www.meetup.com/ishahealthketamineintegrationcircle/)

Fees: Free

About the circle : 

Our Integration Circle isn't just any meetup; it's a profound gathering designed to help individuals navigate, understand, and weave their ketamine therapy experiences into their daily lives. Here, in a nurturing environment, you can candidly discuss your experiences, share groundbreaking insights, and resonate with stories from others walking a path similar to yours. It's worth emphasizing: while deeply insightful, this is not a psychotherapy session. Instead, we come together as a community to promote mutual understanding and collaborative growth.

**Why should you join us?**

**‍**Diverse Insights: Revel in the richness of diverse ketamine therapy experiences, and see your journey in a new light.Compassionate Community: Find solace in a community that listens, understands, and supports without judgment.Connections that Matter: Form bonds with those who truly 'get it' - individuals who, like you, are navigating the profound changes brought by ketamine therapy.Maximize Your Experience: With collective wisdom, enhance your post-therapy integration process and tap into the deeper benefits of your ketamine journey.Join us, and let's explore the vast tapestry of our shared experiences, building bridges of understanding and enlightenment.

## 2\. Psychedelic Integration Circle

Host: San Francisco Psychedelic Society

When: 6 pm - 8 pm PST on the First Tuesday of every month

How often: Monthly

Where: Virtual

Where to sign up: [https://psychedelicsocietysf.org/events/](https://psychedelicsocietysf.org/events/)

Fees: Donation is encouraged

About the circle : 

How can we find the diamond in the shadow? How can we explore and integrate our wounded self with the help of psychedelics?

If you've had a psychedelic experience (including near-death, meditation, and other spiritual experiences) that you are struggling to make sense of, this integration circle provides an opportunity to share, explore, and understand its impact as a community. The circle will be facilitated by psychologists and counsellors with extensive experience in clinical, shamanic, and indigenous healing practices. We will provide tools to go on a self-exploration journey and discover the diamonds in the shadow all together! ---⁣

This donation-based circle will begin with an expansion meditation. It will be followed by a 5-10 min educational presentation about a specific framework in an open discussion format, and it will continue to hold the space for sharing, questions, and feedback.

Please bring a journal, a pen, and any other sacred items you’d like to place on the virtual altar.

Facilitated by Sonia Troiani Doulton and Howard Whitehouse

Registering for our donation-based events grants you a subscription to our email list. You can unsubscribe at any time. Thank you for being a part of our healing community!

## 5\. Women's Online Integration Circle

Host: Tam Integration

When: 6 pm PST on Thursdays

Where: Virtual

How often: Weekly

Focus: Women

Where to sign up: [https://us02web.zoom.us/meeting/register/tZIlf-ihqz0uHNztF1AbQB40DsytCTgzJxbY?\_x\_zm\_rtaid=JZE5Ok68QWC6tBtFf7Q\_3w.1679461275338.b477105353e92da38a6c9847b6f358cb&\_x\_zm\_rhtaid=298](https://us02web.zoom.us/meeting/register/tZIlf-ihqz0uHNztF1AbQB40DsytCTgzJxbY?_x_zm_rtaid=JZE5Ok68QWC6tBtFf7Q_3w.1679461275338.b477105353e92da38a6c9847b6f358cb&_x_zm_rhtaid=298)

Fees : Donation is encouraged

About the circle : 

Psychedelic integration circles can be a remarkable space to meet with like-minded people. We all bring a piece of the puzzle, and sharing our experiences allows us all to grow. Simply by being present and being ourselves, we realize that we have so much to offer. Coming together in a spirited way to unpack beautiful, insightful, terrifying, confusing, and magical experiences. We get a chance to raise the vibration and the quality of connection for us, the community, and the world.

Mt. Tam’s Integration Circles and Educational events seek to be a welcoming space for people who want to discuss entheogenic use in a healthy way, for psychological healing and spiritual growth.

When we have an intense or profound psychedelic experience, it can be hard to describe, especially when we don’t have anyone to talk about it with. Giving voice to our experience with like-minded people can enable us to put things in perspective, gain new insight, and find a resolution.

By listening to other’s experiences, we can learn about how they try to ensure safe and healthy use, gathering a collection of tools and best practices.

  

All of our Circles give people an opportunity to meditate together, share their stories, ask questions, give and receive feedback. We are excited to hear all of your voices and look forward to learning from all of you.

## 7\. QTBIPOC Integration Circle

Host: Psychedelic Liberation Collective

When: 6 pm PST on the First Thursday of the month

How often: Monthly

Focus: QTBIPOC 

Where: Virtual

Where to sign up: https://psychedelicliberationcollective.com/events/qtbipoc-integration-circle-rexpg-5ejah-aznpm

Fees: Donation is encouraged

About the circle : 

Monthly, donation based, virtual circle for folks who identify as QTBIPOC (2SLGBTQIA+ _and_ Black, Indigenous, People of Color) to engage in psychedelic integration work.

Integration is a vital component of the entheogenic experience, bridging the ineffable elements of the medicine experience with one’s daily life. In this space, we begin with establishing space agreements, share a brief grounding practice (somatic, visualization and/or guided meditation), go in a round of introductions, and then invite participants to share their experiences in a peer-supported environment and a lightly facilitated container.  

Registration is offered on a donation basis, suggested donation is $7-20.
