However, this option may not be accessible to everyone due to financial or cultural barriers. To support a wider range of individuals, we've compiled information about online integration circles that operate on a donation basis.

### **What Are Integration Circles and Why Are They Helpful?**

Integration circles are supportive group gatherings where individuals can share, explore, and process their psychedelic experiences. These circles provide a safe and inclusive space to discuss personal insights, challenges, and questions that arise from psychedelic therapy or other profound experiences.

**Why Are Integration Circles Helpful?**

1.  **Community Support:** Integration circles offer a sense of belonging by connecting you with others who understand the transformative nature of psychedelic experiences. Sharing your journey with others can be deeply validating and healing.
2.  **New Perspectives:** Listening to others’ stories can provide fresh insights and help you reframe your own experiences in a constructive way.
3.  **Emotional Processing:** These circles allow you to process emotions, thoughts, and memories in a supportive, non-judgmental environment.
4.  **Tools for Growth:** Facilitators often share frameworks, tools, and techniques for integrating experiences into daily life, enhancing the long-term benefits of therapy.
5.  **Accessibility:** Many integration circles operate on a donation basis, making them a more affordable option for those unable to access traditional psychotherapy.

By joining an integration circle, you can deepen your understanding of your journey, connect with others, and create lasting positive changes in your life.
