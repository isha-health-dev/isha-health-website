At Isha Health, we are committed to providing high-quality healthcare that is accessible to everyone. Since our inception in 2021, we have worked diligently to maintain stable pricing despite the challenges posed by fluctuating economic conditions. Today, we need to share some important updates regarding our pricing structure that will take effect starting June 1, 2024.

### Understanding the Changes

Starting June 1, 2024, the fees for initial consultations and follow-up appointments for patients who begin their treatment on or after this date will be adjusted to $420. We want to emphasize that this adjustment will only affect new patients. All existing patients will continue to enjoy our current rate of $398 for all appointments.

### Why the Change?

Our clinic was founded with the vision of making healthcare accessible, and we've always prioritized this in our pricing strategy. However, the economic landscape has shifted significantly in recent years, impacting the costs associated with providing the highest standard of care.

These changes are essential to help us manage the rising operational costs and continue offering the exceptional service you've come to expect from us. It is a necessary step to sustain the quality of our services without compromising the care we provide.

### What This Means for You

If you or someone you know has been considering starting treatment with us, we encourage taking advantage of the current lower rate before the new pricing takes effect. It's an opportunity to access our services at a more favorable price, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

### We're Here to Help

We understand that changes in pricing can be a concern, and we assure you that this decision was made with careful consideration and a commitment to our patients' needs. We value the trust you place in us and are grateful for your understanding as we navigate these changes.

If you have any questions or need further clarification, please feel free to reach out. Our team is ready to assist and provide any additional information you may need.

Thank you for your continued trust and support in Isha Health. We look forward to continuing to serve you with the same level of dedication and care that you deserve.

Warm regards,

Team Isha Health
